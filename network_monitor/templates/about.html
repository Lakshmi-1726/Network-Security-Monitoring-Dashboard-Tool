About NetSec Monitor

NetSec Monitor is a lightweight and real-time security monitoring platform designed to help organizations track security events, user activity, and potential threats with ease.

Our Mission

We aim to provide an intuitive and affordable platform for monitoring network activity, enabling teams to quickly detect anomalies, investigate suspicious behavior, and respond to alerts efficiently.

What NetSec Monitor Offers

  • Real-time Event Tracking
  • Smart Alert System
  • Dashboard & Analytics
  • CSV Data Export
  • User-friendly Interface

Who Uses This Platform?

  • Small & medium businesses
  • Educational institutions
  • Cybersecurity students & researchers
  • System administrators
  • Home labs & personal networks

We continue to improve the platform with new features and refinements. Your feedback plays a crucial role in shaping future updates.
